The Science

Why Microgreens?

Microgreens are young vegetable greens harvested just after the first leaves develop. Despite their tiny size, they deliver an outsized nutritional punch that scientists are only beginning to fully understand.

  • Up to 40x More Nutrients

    Research shows microgreens contain significantly higher concentrations of vitamins and minerals compared to their mature counterparts.

  • Rich in Antioxidants

    High levels of polyphenols and carotenoids help fight inflammation and support your immune system.

  • Easy to Use

    Add them to salads, smoothies, sandwiches, soups, or use as a beautiful garnish. No cooking required.

  • Sustainable Growing

    Microgreens use 95% less water and space than traditional farming, making them one of the most eco-friendly foods.
